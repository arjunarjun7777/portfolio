About Me

Know more about who I am

Hello! I'm B. Hema Arjun, a 21-year-old passionate learner and aspiring tech innovator currently in my third year of B.Tech in Computer Science at Raghu Engineering College, specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.

I’m fascinated by how technology solves real-world problems. My interests lie in web development, Python programming, machine learning, and graphic design. I combine creativity with logic to deliver thoughtful and impactful digital solutions.

I have experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, Data Structures, and AI/ML. I’m currently learning the MERN stack to become a full-stack web developer. I enjoy designing and developing responsive, user-friendly web applications.

As a freelance graphic designer, I’ve worked on logo design, branding, social media creatives, and UI mockups. Freelancing has improved my communication, time management, and design thinking skills.

Outside tech, I enjoy playing cricket, gaming, and traveling. These hobbies inspire creativity and bring balance to my work-life.

I’m open to internships, freelance opportunities, and collaborative projects that help me grow and make a real impact. Let’s connect and build something awesome together!
