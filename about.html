About Me

Hello! I'm B. Hema Arjun, a 21-year-old passionate learner and aspiring tech innovator currently in my third year of B.Tech in Computer Science at Raghu Engineering College, specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.

From the beginning of my academic journey, I've been fascinated by the endless possibilities of technology and its power to solve real-world problems. This interest has guided me to dive deep into web development, Python programming, machine learning, and graphic designing. I take pride in combining analytical thinking with creative expression, which allows me to bring unique and thoughtful solutions to every project I take on.

Technically, I’m well-versed in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, AI/ML algorithms, and Data Structures, and I'm actively learning the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js) to become a proficient full-stack developer. I love building intuitive, user-friendly websites and intelligent applications that make a difference.

On the creative side, I’m a freelance graphic designer, experienced in logo design, social media creatives, branding assets, and UI mockups. Through freelancing, I’ve not only honed my design skills but also developed client communication, time management, and business acumen.

Outside the world of code and design, I’m someone who believes in balance and fun. In my free time, I enjoy playing cricket, gaming, and traveling to new places. These hobbies keep me energized, open-minded, and inspired — always ready to take on new challenges with a fresh perspective.

I’m currently looking for internship opportunities, freelance collaborations, or real-world tech projects where I can contribute meaningfully and grow professionally. I believe in continuous learning, teamwork, and creating solutions that are both functional and impactful. Let’s connect and create something great together!
